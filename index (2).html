Sunday Morning
Lower Body + Prehab
60 minutes. Prehab first, every session — non-negotiable given your hamstring and calf history.
Progression: Wks 1–4: learn the movements, hit the starting weights. Wks 5–8: add weight/reps every 2 weeks. Wks 9–11: push hard — peak strength phase. Wks 12–15: reduce to 80% of peak weight, maintain only.
Prehab — Do these FIRST, every session ⌄
Calf Raise Machine — straight leg
Slow 3-second lowering phase. Full range — all the way up, all the way down. Start at 46kg. Add 4kg when 15 reps feels comfortable (every 2–3 weeks).
3 × 15
Start: 46kg
Calf Raise Machine — bent knee (soleus)
Slight knee bend targets soleus — the calf muscle most involved in running-related strains. Same machine, reduce weight slightly. 3-second lowering phase.
3 × 15
Start: 36kg
Hamstring Curl Machine
Start conservatively — find a weight where reps 10–12 are hard but form stays clean. Slow 3-second lowering phase. This is your hamstring injury prevention anchor. Add weight gradually each week once you know your baseline.
3 × 12
Start: test first session
Main Lifts ⌄
Leg Press
Feet shoulder-width, mid-height on plate. Full range — knees to 90°. Don't lock out at the top. Primary quad and glute builder. Add 10kg every 2–3 weeks as strength builds.

Wk 9+: 65kg
Leg Extension Machine
Full range, controlled lowering (2–3 sec). Isolates the quad. Pairs well with leg press. Do not rush these — the eccentric phase is where the strength gains are.

Romanian Deadlift (Dumbbells)
Hinge at hips, soft knee. Lower dumbbells along the legs until you feel a deep hamstring stretch. Slow and controlled. Pairs with the hamstring curl for full hamstring coverage. Start with a weight you can control perfectly.

Dumbbell Hip Thrust (floor or bench)

Tuesday Night
Upper Body + Core
60 minutes — no heavy leg work. Chest, back, shoulders, arms, core.
Progression: Wks 1–4: learn the movements at starting weights, 3 sets. Wks 5–11: add weight every 2 weeks, 3–4 sets — this is where the visible changes happen. Wks 12–15: drop to 80% of peak weight, maintain only. Rest 60–90 sec between sets.

Macro Targets ⌄
Protein
160g / day
2g per kg. Non-negotiable at 49 — protects muscle mass and supports gym adaptation. 40g across 4 meals. Chicken, fish, eggs, Greek yogurt, shake.
Carbohydrates
5–7g/kg hard days
400–560g on Tue and Sat. Drop to 3–4g/kg on rest and easy days. Oats, rice, pasta, bread, fruit, potatoes.
Fat
70–90g / day
Do not go low fat — suppresses testosterone at 49. Avocado, olive oil, nuts, eggs, salmon.

Long Run Nutrition Protocol ⌄
Night before
Carb-heavy dinner. Pasta, rice, potato. No alcohol. Early bed.
Morning of
Oats + banana + coffee. 90 min before. Same every week — becomes race morning routine.
During
Gel at 45 min, every 30–35 min after. Water with each gel. Salt tab at 90 min+ if warm.
Within 30 min after
Shake + banana or chocolate milk. 40g protein + fast carbs. Do not skip.
